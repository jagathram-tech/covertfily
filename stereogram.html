How to see a Magic Eye

Method 1: Parallel Viewing (Look Through)

  1. Bring your face very close to the screen, almost touching it.
  2. Try to look through the image into the distance behind your screen. Your vision should blur.
  3. Slowly move your head straight back, away from the screen, keeping your eyes relaxed and unfocused.
  4. At a certain distance, the hidden 3D image will suddenly snap into focus!

Method 2: Cross-Eyed Viewing

  1. Hold a pen or finger halfway between your eyes and the screen.
  2. Focus your eyes purely on the pen/finger.
  3. While keeping focus on the pen, pay attention to the blurred image in the background.
  4. The repeating patterns will overlap. Once they lock together, move the pen away and enjoy the 3D shape!
