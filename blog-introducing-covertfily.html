Introducing Covertfily: The MIT-Licensed Open Source Conversion Suite

By Covertfily Team • April 9, 2026

Today marks the official introduction of Covertfily, a project born out of a simple need: a file converter that respects its users. By releasing our code under the MIT License, we are inviting the world to audit, improve, and use our tools without restriction.

Built for Developers and Users Alike

Most conversion tools are either bloated paid software or ad-riddled websites with questionable privacy practices. Covertfily is different. We built it with a clean, modern interface and a "no-nonsense" philosophy. No account needed. No subscription required. Just pick your files and convert.

A Modern Technology Stack

Covertfily isn't just a Wrapper. It intelligently combines the best open-source libraries available today:

  • jsPDF & PDF-lib: For lightning-fast PDF generation and manipulation.
  • PDF.js: For high-fidelity browser-side rendering.
  • SheetJS & JSZip: For complex data and archive handling.

Transparency as a Feature

We believe that when it comes to your files, transparency is a requirement, not a bonus. Because our code is open source, anyone can see exactly how their data is handled. We don't hide behind proprietary algorithms; we show the world how local-first technology can be both powerful and private.
