Are my files safe?
Yes, absolutely. Unlike other converters, Covertfily processes your files entirely within your web browser. Your data is never uploaded to any server, making it 100% private.
Do I need an internet connection?
Only to load the website initially. Once the page is loaded, the conversion logic runs locally on your computer, meaning you could even disconnect from the internet and it would still work.
Is Covertfily really free?
Yes, Covertfily is an open-source project licensed under MIT. It is free to use for everyone, forever, without any hidden costs or subscriptions.
