Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2026

Our Privacy Philosophy

At Covertfily, we believe your data belongs to you. That's why we built a platform where your files never leave your computer.

1. Zero Data Collection

Unlike traditional online converters, Covertfily does not use any servers to process your files. All conversion logic is written in JavaScript and WebAssembly, which executes locally within your web browser's sandbox. We do not, and cannot, see your files, their contents, or their metadata.

Key takeaway: Your files are processed in your RAM, not on our servers. When you close the tab, the data is gone forever.

2. No Account Required

You can use every single feature of Covertfily without ever creating an account. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers.

3. Third-Party Analytics

We use Google Analytics (gtag.js) to understand general traffic patterns, such as which tools are most popular and which browsers are being used. This information is anonymized and does not include any data related to the files you process.

4. Cookies

We may use minimal cookies or local storage strictly for functional purposes, such as remembering your theme preference or tool settings. These cookies do not track you across other websites.

5. Open Source Transparency

To prove our commitment to privacy, the entire source code of Covertfily is available on GitHub. Anyone can inspect our code to verify that no data is being uploaded or transmitted to external servers.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Since we don't have your contact information, we encourage you to check this page periodically for updates.
