Secure PDF to Word Conversion in Your Browser

Welcome to the safest way to convert your PDF files to editable Word Documents (.docx). Unlike most free online tools that force you to upload your sensitive files to an unknown cloud server, Covertfily's PDF to Word converter processes everything 100% locally in your web browser.

PDF (Portable Document Format) is the gold standard for sharing documents exactly as they were designed to be seen. However, editing a PDF can be notoriously difficult. By converting your PDF to a Word document, you regain the ability to easily edit the text, change formatting, and update the document's content using Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or LibreOffice.

How Our PDF Extractor Works

This tool utilizes a client-side JavaScript engine to read the internal structure of your PDF file. It carefully scans each page, extracts the text layers, and then constructs a brand new DOCX file containing that text using the powerful `docx.js` library. Because this happens entirely on your own device's CPU and memory, the conversion is both blazingly fast and completely private.

Key Benefits

  • Absolute Privacy: Your financial documents, legal contracts, and personal files never leave your computer.
  • No Size Limits Imposed: We don't restrict file sizes or put you in a queue. It's limited only by your browser's capabilities.
  • Instant Downloads: As soon as the conversion finishes in your browser, the file is instantly ready to save.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my files uploaded to your servers?

No. We guarantee absolute privacy. The conversion algorithm runs locally within your browser using JavaScript. No file data is ever transmitted to us or any third party.

Will it preserve images and complex formatting?

Our current client-side engine focuses primarily on extracting text and maintaining basic paragraph structures. Highly complex formatting, tables, and images from the PDF may not be perfectly replicated in the resulting Word document.

What if my PDF is a scanned image?

This tool extracts embedded text layers from digital PDFs. If your PDF is a scanned document without an invisible text layer, this tool will output a blank document. For scanned documents, please use our dedicated OCR (Image to Text) tool instead.
